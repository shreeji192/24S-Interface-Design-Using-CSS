History

Milky Way is a delicious chocolate bar created in 1923 by Frank C. Mars and originally manufactured in Minneapolis, Minnesota. It was named after a popular malted milk drink of the day, not the astronomical galaxy.

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Corn Syrup
  • Hydrogenated Palm Kernel Oil
  • Skim Milk
  • Less than 2% - Lactose, Salt, Egg Whites, Artificial Flavor
